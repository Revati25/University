Institute of Technology and Management (ITM) Business School is a top-ranked institute for thousands of students coming across the globe. It is a private autonomous institute that was established in 1991 and is located in Navi Mumbai, Maharashtra.

ITM Business School, Navi Mumbai is providing only full-time PGDM programs in various specializations.

Aspirants who are looking to develop their passion in the future, the ITM Business School is the perfect choice for them to study PGDM in Mumbai.

ITM Business School is the top choice for students who aspire to make an impact in their careers. The college provides cutting-edge infrastructure, top-class infrastructure, a career-focused curriculum, and top-notch job offers from over 650 recruiters which include India’s top brands.

How to Take Admission in ITM Business School for PGDM?

The admissions process is quick and straightforward to help guide you through the process:


Step 1: Qualification

  1. Candidates must score an average of 50% marks in graduation from any AIU recognised college & qualifying marks in an MBA entrance examination.

Step 2: Application

  • * The students looking to study PGDM in Mumbai should apply online on the official website- forms.itm.edu/pgdm

Step 3: Shortlisting

  1. The applicants who are selected are asked to attend the micro presentations and personal interviews process selection rounds

Step 4: Admissions

  1. After completing micro presentations and personal interviews, the students may be provided admission to a course that is based on overall performance in the selection process.
